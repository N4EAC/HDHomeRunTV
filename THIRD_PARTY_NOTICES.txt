HDHomeRun TV Player uses mpv as a separate portable playback process.

mpv project: https://mpv.io/
Windows build origin: https://github.com/shinchiro/mpv-winbuild-cmake
Pinned build: mpv-x86_64-20260610-git-304426c.7z

The player downloads mpv directly from the build publisher on first playback and verifies:
SHA-256 facac536baa73c7b925771af5e39a3c9cb16b8d75b59a6e9800de89799dffca7

Corresponding source and build information are available from the upstream mpv and
mpv-winbuild-cmake projects.
